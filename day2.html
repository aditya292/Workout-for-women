Day 2: Cardio & Core

Get ready to sweat, smile, and strengthen your core! 🌟
(Repeat the circuit 3 times)

🌸 Jumping Jacks

Duration: 45 seconds
Jump, stretch, and energize your whole body like a star! ✨

🌸 High Knees

Duration: 45 seconds
Run in place lifting those knees high — feel the energy rise! ⚡

🌸 Mountain Climbers

Duration: 45 seconds
Climb your way to strength — one powerful move at a time! 🏔️

🌸 Bicycle Crunches

Reps: 20
Twist and pedal those legs — feel your core ignite! 🔥

🌸 Russian Twists

Reps: 20
Tap side to side and build that lovely waistline! 🌷

"Small steps every day lead to big changes! 💪 Keep moving, beautiful!"
