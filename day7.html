Day 7: Rest or Gentle Yoga

Take a step back, relax, and rejuvenate your body with some gentle yoga. 🧘‍♀️

🌸 Child's Pose (Balasana)

Duration: 1-2 minutes
Relax your body by sitting on your heels and stretching forward, releasing tension in your back and shoulders. 🧘‍♀️

🌸 Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Duration: 1-2 minutes
Lift your hips up to the sky and press your heels down to stretch the hamstrings, shoulders, and back. 🐕

🌸 Cat-Cow Pose (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana)

Duration: 1-2 minutes
Move between arching and rounding your back to release tension and improve spinal flexibility. 🐱🐮

🌸 Seated Forward Fold (Paschimottanasana)

Duration: 1-2 minutes
Stretch your legs out and fold forward, feeling the lengthening of your spine and hamstrings. 🙏

🌸 Savasana (Corpse Pose)

Duration: 5-10 minutes
Rest in a lying position, focus on your breath, and let your body absorb the benefits of your practice. 🛌

"Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self. 🌸"
🏠

Benefits of Doing Yoga:

  • Improves flexibility and balance.
  • Reduces stress and anxiety through mindful breathing.
  • Enhances mental clarity and focus.
  • Strengthens muscles and increases stamina.
  • Promotes overall relaxation and recovery.
  • Improves posture and spinal health.
