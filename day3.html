Day 3: Lower Body Strength

It's leg day! Strong legs are the foundation of your fitness journey. 🦵

🌸 Sumo Squats

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15
Stand wide, lower your hips deep, and feel that inner thigh burn! 🔥

🌸 Step-ups

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12 per leg
Step up with power, engage those legs, and feel the strength build! 💪

🌸 Wall Sit

Duration: 45 seconds x 3
Hold your body strong against the wall — feel the fire in your legs! 🔥

🌸 Calf Raises

Sets: 3 | Reps: 20
Lift those heels up high, feel the calves getting stronger with each rep! 🌟

🌸 Glute Kickbacks

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15 per leg
Kick it back, squeeze that booty, and feel the burn in your glutes! 🍑
