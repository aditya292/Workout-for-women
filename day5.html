Day 5: Upper Body Strength

Strengthen and tone your arms and shoulders with just your bodyweight! 💪

🌸 Push-ups

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-15
Lower your body with control and push up to build your chest and arms. 💥

🌸 Tricep Dips (Using a Chair or Bench)

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12
Dip down to work those triceps, using just your body weight and a chair for support. 💪

🌸 Diamond Push-ups

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-10
Form a diamond shape with your hands and lower your chest to focus on your triceps and chest. 💎

🌸 Plank to Push-up

Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-12
Start in a plank position, then push up into a push-up position and back. Engages core and arms! 🏋️‍♀️

🌸 Shoulder Taps

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15 per side
Hold a plank position, and alternate tapping each shoulder — great for shoulders and core! 💪

"Stronger arms, stronger you. Keep pushing — you’ve got this! 💖"
