🏡 5-Minute Home Warm-Up 🏡

Get your body moving and your heart smiling! 🌸

1. March in Place 🏃‍♀️

Duration: 1 minute
Stay light on your feet, swing your arms gently.

2. Standing Arm Circles 💪

Duration: 45 seconds each direction
Make small to big circles with your arms.

3. Standing Hip Openers 🌟

Duration: 45 seconds each side
Lift knee, rotate outward, then back down gently.

4. Standing Side Reaches 🌈

Duration: 1 minute
Stretch side-to-side, reaching arms overhead.

5. Calf Raises 💃

Duration: 1 minute
Slowly lift your heels up and lower back down — great for ankles and calves!

🌟 "Take the first step — even if it's inside your room!" 🌟
