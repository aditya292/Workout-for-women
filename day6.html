Day 6: Low Impact HIIT (Fat Burning)

Effective fat-burning exercises without jumping! 💥

🌸 Bodyweight Squats

Duration: 20 seconds | Rest: 10 seconds
Squat down, keeping your chest lifted, and rise with power to engage your legs and glutes. 🍑

🌸 Standing Side Crunches

Duration: 20 seconds | Rest: 10 seconds
Bring your knee up toward your elbow while engaging your obliques, working your core. 🤸‍♀️

🌸 Glute Bridges

Duration: 20 seconds | Rest: 10 seconds
Lift your hips up to squeeze your glutes and core, engaging your lower body. 🔥

🌸 Modified Plank (Knees on the Ground)

Duration: 20 seconds | Rest: 10 seconds
Hold a modified plank with knees on the ground to target your core and arms without the pressure. 💪

🌸 High Knee March

Duration: 20 seconds | Rest: 10 seconds
March in place, lifting your knees high to engage your core and legs without any impact. 🚶‍♀️

"Burn fat, not joints. Take care of your body while getting stronger each day! 💖"
